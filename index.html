Overview

Baseball is as much a mental game as it is a physical one. Understanding the psychological challenges players face can help improve performance and enjoyment of the game.

Techniques to Improve Mental Strength

  1. Focus and Visualization
    • Imagine successful outcomes.
    • Develop a pre-game routine.
  2. Stress Management
    • Practice deep breathing exercises.
    • Use progressive muscle relaxation.
  3. Positive Reinforcement
    • Use affirmations to build confidence.
    • Set achievable goals.

Education

My name is Jackson Ely and I am a third year college student. My first year of college was attended at Phoenix College. At Phoenix College I tore my MCL which led me to have to focus on the parts of the game that were not physical. I started meditating every day in order to learn how to clear my mind. My ultimate goal was to reach the flow state. After my first year at Phoenix College, I transferred to San Diego City College. Here I learned how to apply the learnings from my days at Phoenix College. I was the only freshman starter at San Diego City and I fully attribute that to the mental aspect of the game. I have learned that if I can clear my mind and focus solely on the present, I can become a better baseball player. This is why I decided to put my focus of the website on the mental aspect of baseball.
